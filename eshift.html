E-Shift Shifting System

Project Description

The proposed software solution for e-Shift is designed to automate and streamline the company's day-to-day operations, focusing on managing customer registrations, job scheduling, and load transportation efficiently.

The system will assign unique identifiers to each customer, job, and load, while tracking key details such as pickup and delivery locations, transport units and load statuses.

By digitizing these processes, the solution aims to enhance operational efficiency, improve customer service, and ensure accurate record-keeping for all household goods transportation activities.

The application will provide a user-friendly interface for staff to manage jobs, allocate resources, and monitor progress, ensuring seamless coordination from pickup to delivery.

Key Requirements

  • Customer registration and authentication with unique IDs
  • Job creation and management with unique job/load tracking
  • Transport unit allocation (lorry, driver, assistant, container)
  • Real time status updates
  • Automated notifications for customers and staff
  • Admin dashboard for managing jobs, resources and reports
