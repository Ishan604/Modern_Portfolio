The Crown Stays Hotel Reservation System

Project Description

The proposed Hotel Reservation System is designed to automate and streamline the hotelâ€™s booking operations, focusing on managing room reservations, guest check-ins, and billing processes efficiently.

The system will assign unique identifiers to each reservation and guest, while tracking key details such as room types, occupancy, arrival/departure dates, and payment status.

By digitizing these processes, the solution aims to enhance booking accuracy, improve guest satisfaction, and ensure seamless management of room availability and revenue tracking.

The application will provide a user-friendly interface for guests to make, modify, or cancel reservations online, while enabling staff to manage check-ins, no-show billing, and daily occupancy reports for optimized operations.

Key Requirements

  • Guest registration and authentication with unique IDs
  • Room reservation management with booking IDs
  • Room type categorization and availability tracking
  • Check-in/check-out automation with real-time updates
  • Payment processing (credit card holds, no-show billing)
  • Automated notifications for bookings and reminders
  • Admin dashboard for managing rooms, reservations, and reports
  • Daily occupancy and revenue reporting
