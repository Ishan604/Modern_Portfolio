Educational Council Chatbot

Project Description

The proposed Educational Council Chatbot is designed to streamline academic support and administrative guidance, focusing on delivering instant assistance to students, parents, and educators through AI-powered interactions.

The system will provide personalized responses to queries while tracking key details such as academic progress, career guidance, institutional policies, and resource recommendations.

By leveraging conversational AI, the solution aims to enhance accessibility to educational resources, improve decision-making efficiency, and reduce response times for common inquiries.

The chatbot will offer a user-friendly interface for real-time Q&A, while enabling administrators to update knowledge bases, monitor usage trends, and continuously improve service quality through feedback integration.

Key Requirements

  • User authentication with role-based access
  • Natural Language Processing for understanding academic queries
  • Knowledge base integration for curriculum and policy information
  • Career guidance module with personalized recommendations
  • Academic progress tracking and reporting
  • Institution policy and deadline reminders
  • Admin dashboard for content management and analytics
  • Feedback collection and continuous learning system
